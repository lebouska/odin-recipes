Oven Fried Chicken

Description

Easy to make and amazing fried chicken made on an oven.

Ingredients

  • 12 chicken thighs, but you can also use breasts, legs, wings or even drumsticks
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup Italian seasoned bread crumbs
  • 3 eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/2 cup vegetal Oil, but you can also use canola or olive oil.

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 °F or 175 °C
  2. Place flour in a shallow plate or bowl and season with salt and pepper to taste. Put bread crumbs in another shallow plate or bowl and beat eggs in another bowl.
  3. Dredge chicken piece by piece in the flour, then the egg, then the bread crumbs, until all pieces are coated.
  4. Pour oil into a large baking dish; add the breaded chicken thighs and sprinkle with paprika to taste.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes, then turn pieces over and bake for another 30 minutes. Remove from the oven and drain in paper towels.
